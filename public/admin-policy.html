🔒 Admin Access Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

This Admin Access Policy ("Policy") governs the access, handling, and protection of user personal data by administrative personnel ("Admins") of HeartConnect. Compliance with this Policy is mandatory for all Admins.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to all employees, contractors, and volunteers who have administrative access to the HeartConnect backend systems, including but not limited to the admin dashboard, databases, and logging systems.

2. Purpose

The purpose of this Policy is to:

  • Protect the privacy and security of user data (including IP addresses, location, social links, and verification status).
  • Ensure that Admin access is limited to legitimate business purposes (fraud investigation, support, and compliance).
  • Prevent misuse or unauthorized disclosure of user data.
  • Establish legal accountability for internal data breaches.

3. Access Controls

  • Role‑Based Access: Admin permissions are granted only to the minimum extent necessary for job functions.
  • Multi‑Factor Authentication (MFA): All Admin accounts must have MFA enabled.
  • Individual Accounts: Admin accounts are non‑shareable. Each Admin must use their own unique credentials.

4. Mandatory Logging of Admin Actions

Every Admin action that involves viewing or modifying user data must be logged with the following details:

  • Admin user ID
  • Timestamp of access
  • Target user ID (the user whose data was accessed)
  • Action performed (e.g., "viewed IP", "updated verification status", "resolved report")
  • Reason for access (mandatory free‑text field)

Logs shall be retained for a minimum of 12 months and are subject to periodic audits.

5. Data Handling Rules

Admins may access user data only for legitimate platform operations, including:

  • Investigating fraud or impersonation reports.
  • Responding to user support requests.
  • Enforcing platform policies and legal compliance.

Admins must not:

  • Share, screenshot, or transmit user data outside the Platform.
  • Access data out of personal curiosity or for non‑business reasons.
  • Use user data for any purpose unrelated to their job duties.
  • Leave sensitive data visible on screens when unattended.

Admins must report any suspected data breach or misuse immediately to the designated security officer.

⚠️ Legal Liability for Internal Breaches

Any Admin who intentionally or negligently accesses, discloses, or misuses user data in violation of this Policy shall be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination and legal liability. Personal liability may arise under applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, PIPL, etc.) and company policies.

6. Audit and Review

The company reserves the right to conduct regular audits of Admin access logs. Unexplained or excessive access will be investigated. All Admins are required to cooperate with any internal or external investigation.

7. Acknowledgment and Training

All Admins must sign an acknowledgment of this Policy before receiving access. Periodic training on data privacy and security best practices is mandatory.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be updated from time to time. Admins will be notified of material changes and must review and acknowledge the revised version.
