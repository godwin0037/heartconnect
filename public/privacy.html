What data we collect

We collect the information you provide when creating an account (name, age, gender, language, country, profile photo, and social links), messages you send through customer support chat, and technical data such as your IP address and, if you choose to share it, your precise device location.

How we use your data

We use this information to operate the platform, verify account authenticity, prevent fraud and impersonation, respond to support requests, and enforce our Terms of Service. Location data feeds into our automated risk-scoring system, which helps us flag suspicious accounts.

Your rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time from your account Settings. Deleting your account removes your profile, likes, and reports, and is logged internally for audit purposes.

Data retention

Account and profile data is retained while your account is active. Deletion records (username and reason) are retained for a limited period for fraud-prevention and legal-compliance purposes.

Security measures

Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Access to the admin dashboard is role-restricted and every admin action is logged.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the in-app support chat.
