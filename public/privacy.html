💕 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Welcome to HeartConnect (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account registration: username, email address, password.
  • Profile information: name, age, gender, language, country, and profile picture.
  • Social media links: URLs you submit (WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, etc.).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP Address and Geolocation: We collect your IP address and derive your approximate location (country and city) when you sign up, log in, and interact with the Platform. This is processed for security, fraud prevention, and to enforce regional legal requirements.
  • Device and Browser Data: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and referring URLs.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and actions taken on the Platform.

1.3 Information from Social Media (OAuth)

If you choose to verify your social media accounts via OAuth, we receive your unique social ID, public profile information, and the date your social account was created. We do not store your social media passwords.

1.4 Precise Location (Optional)

If you grant your browser permission, we may collect your precise GPS location. You are not required to provide this, and you can withdraw consent at any time via your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account creation and authentication – Contract performance
  • Fraud prevention and security (IP/location checks) – Legitimate interest
  • Displaying your profile to other users – Consent
  • Admin review of flagged accounts – Legitimate interest
  • Sending service notifications – Contract performance
  • Improving our Platform – Legitimate interest

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Other Users: Your profile information (name, age, gender, language, country, and social links) is visible to other registered users.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third‑party vendors who help us operate the Platform (hosting, analytics, email delivery). They are contractually bound to process data only for our specified purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our Platform, users, or others.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. IP and location logs are retained for 90 days for security audits.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Opt‑out of “sale” (we do not sell data)

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@heartconnect.com.

6. Security Measures

We implement industry‑standard security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews. All admin access to user data is logged and audited.

7. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

8. Children’s Privacy

Our Platform is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the “Last Updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@heartconnect.com.
