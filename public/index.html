🔒 Why We Verify

To keep our community safe from fake accounts and impersonation.

We collect and verify your social media links (via OAuth) to prove that you are a real person and not someone pretending to be another user.

What we do with your data:

  • Your social ID is stored securely to confirm ownership.
  • Your IP address and approximate location are used to detect suspicious logins (e.g., logging in from two different countries within a short time).
  • This data is never shared with other users – it's only used by our security system and admins to prevent fraud.

You can revoke OAuth permissions at any time via your social platform settings.
